    ABOUT JEFF’S ART & BOOKS

    Jeffers Joseph is an artist and an author. The art of Drawing always came naturally which explains why Jeff has been drawing since childhood. Writing was another natural talent and was further developed through the years which ultimately led to his first published novel ‘Money, Misery & Murder’ (Murder and Mystery) in 2011. This was later followed up by two other published books - ‘Friends with Deficits’(Romance and Suspense); and ‘Democracy’s Hypocrisies’ (Politics).

    Jeff is currently in the process of publishing two additional books:

    ‘Their Lives Mattered’ (Social Justice) — a book of art and commentary, in addition to an informative and interactive book titled, ‘A Coloring Book of Black Inventors’. With an innate artistic talent, Jeffers has developed into an accomplished artist in many areas of the industry. Much of his work is on display on this website.
